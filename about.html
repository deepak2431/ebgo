Profile

The humble entrant in EPC industry, Engineer Brothers Group of Construction (EBGO) is an example of acumen. His natural business instincts with an eye on the future have made EBGO a name to be reckoned with in the industry. Mr. Nirbhay Bharadwaj, Mr. Mithelesh Kumar, Mr. Vikas Kumar Ray upholds strong Professional Values & solid business skills. His business acumen was build up on his underlying belief in commitment and people, leading to a totally committed work force. According to him, the fundamental reason of success lies in, absolute transparent business deals, will power and people, where all of the partners can succeed only when each takes care of the others interest. 

EBGO Group is a group of Structural Engineer with over 05 years of diversified experience, always believed in the vast opportunity poised by India’s growth story and after gaining industry experience from his stint with number of Consulting firm for 02 years, he turned towards his passion of transforming Infrastructure Industry and laid the foundation for EBGO in the year 2019.


In the early years, company started with a humble beginning of Structural works & Earth works in Nagpur . Over the period, company expanded its footprint across Mumbai Region under the aegis of our their Partners and started executing.

Who we are now?

Today, is an ISO 9004:2018 Certified engineering conglomerate specializing in Highways, Railways, RWD, PWD (End to End) solutions, having 2 branches across India; employing more than 20 people, completed 10 projects and has a turnover of 150 Million. EBGO’s endeavours have been so successful that within a short span of 05 years, more than 10 projects have been delivered successfully, including PNC, TTIPL, Malhotra Build Cona, Megha Ltd, RSIEL, IRCON, VK Enterprise and name a few.


We value integrity, trust, service and respect for individuals and for the environment. We pride ourselves for being one of India’s emerging organizations in EPC Industry known for our above par project management capabilities and customer satisfaction besides being recognized for our fair, transparent and ethical dealings.


Our people are our assets, who has contributed for the exponential growth till date. The management teams comprise of highly-motivated, industry and domain experts who have been with the organization, in most cases, for over a decade now. They closely drive and lead their respective functions infusing impetus, passion, energy and growth for their teams and the organization at large.
